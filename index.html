I'm a Mathematics and Physics student at The University of Manchester, focusing on Probability & Statistics and Theoretical Quantum Mechanics. I'm also a Research Assistant at Harvard University, working in the Statistical Reinforcement Learning Laboratory and supervised by Dr. Shuangning Li & Prof. Susan Murphy.

This past summer I worked as a Research Assistant at The University of Manchester under the supervision of Dr. Robert Gaunt, researching the generalized hyperbolic (GH) distribution. In the past, I have completed internships in Data Science and Machine Learning, and have been invited to insight events at Jane Street and Citadel. I'm from Barcelona, where I graduated from the Spanish Baccalaureate in 2020.

My research interests are varied and encompass machine learning, reinforcement learning, optimization, regression, inference, stochastic processes and forecasting.
