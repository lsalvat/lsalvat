About

Welcome to my website! I'm a Mathematics and Physics student at The University of Manchester, focusing on Probability & Statistics and Theoretical Quantum Mechanics. I'm also an incoming Research Assistant at Harvard University, working in the Statistical Reinforcement Learning Laboratory and supervised by Dr. Shuangning Li & Prof. Susan Murphy.

This past summer I worked as a Research Assistant at The University of Manchester under the supervision of Dr. Robert Gaunt, researching the generalized hyperbolic (GH) distribution. In the past I have completed internships in Data Science and Machine Learning, and have been invited to insight events at Jane Street and Citadel. I'm originally from Barcelona, where I graduated from the Spanish Baccalaureate with "Matrícula d'Honor" (Distinction) in 2020.

My reseach interests encompass machine learning, reinforcement learning, optimization, regression, inference, stochastic processes and forecasting.

My hobbies include photography, enduro, running, reading philosophy and skiing.
